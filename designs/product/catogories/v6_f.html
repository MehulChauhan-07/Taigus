Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

expand_more

Our standard MOQ starts at 10,000 units for stock series. For custom molds or proprietary IML designs, MOQ typically begins at 50,000 units.

Are your containers microwave and freezer safe?

expand_more

Yes, all our PP-based containers are designed to withstand temperatures from -20°C to +110°C, making them suitable for both deep freezing and microwave reheating.

Can you provide high-barrier IML labels?

expand_more

Absolutely. We offer EVOH-integrated labels that provide superior oxygen and light barriers, significantly extending the shelf life of sensitive products.

Do you offer 100% PCR (Post-Consumer Recycled) options?

expand_more

We offer ISCC Plus certified circular polymers and mass-balanced PCR solutions specifically approved for food-contact applications.

What are the lead times for custom production?

expand_more

Standard production lead times are 3-4 weeks. For new custom molds, lead times are approximately 10-12 weeks from design sign-off to first article production.
