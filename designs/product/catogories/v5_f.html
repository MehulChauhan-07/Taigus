Discover Taigus IML Containers

Our comprehensive portfolio features 11 precision-engineered container sizes across four distinct series. Each unit utilizes advanced In-Mold Labeling (IML) technology, where the label becomes an integral part of the container wall.

This integration offers superior photographic quality, humidity resistance, and complete recyclability, meeting the rigorous demands of the modern food industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? expand_more

Our standard MOQ starts at 5,000 units for stock items. For custom IML printing, the MOQ typically starts at 25,000 units depending on the container size.

Are your containers microwave and freezer safe? expand_more

Yes, all our IML containers are made from high-grade virgin polypropylene, which is inherently freezer-safe down to -20°C and microwave-safe for reheating purposes.

How long does custom IML printing take? expand_more

Once the artwork is approved, production typically takes 15-20 business days. We provide a digital mockup and a physical press proof for approval before full production begins.

Do you provide lid options for all containers? expand_more

Yes, every container series has a matching lid specifically engineered for that shape. We offer snap-fit, tamper-evident, and twist-lock options depending on your product requirements.
